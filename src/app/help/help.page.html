Help Settings Activated Exercise Types

There are different types of exercises. Only exercises of types that you activated will be shown to you.

 Clear Cache

Clearing the cache helps when the concepts on the server have changed and you want to get those changes. By clearing the cache, all concepts have to be fetched again and thereby you get the new concepts.

 Cooldown Time

The cooldown time describes for how long an exercise cannot be presented to you after you have seen the solution. Its purpose is to diversify your learning experience.

 Deactivated Exercises

Deactivated exercises will not be shown to you any more. You can activate them by clicking on the X. You can only deactivate an exercise while it is shown to you during exercise.
